Methodology & Notes
• Methodology: Models contributions that step up annually (matching wage growth) but are contributed evenly each month. Compounds monthly at the nominal rate.
• Return rate: Single nominal rate locked in at current starting age, held constant through accumulation period.
• Target: (incomeReplacement / withdrawalRate) × (1 + inflation)^n.
• Initial investment: Entered in today's dollars, divided by current salary to normalize.
• Displayed values use standard mathematical rounding to the nearest whole percentage. Values > 87.5% are hidden.
